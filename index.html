Our Story

Where Moncton's Spirit Meets the Plate

✦

Maison Beausoleil was born from a simple belief: that the richest stories are told through food. Nestled in the heart of downtown Moncton — a city that thrives on its bilingual soul and Maritime warmth — we honour the Acadian culinary tradition with a contemporary lens.

Our menus change with the seasons, guided by relationships with local farmers across the Tantramar Marshes and fishermen from the Bay of Fundy. Whether it's a Tuesday lobster bisque or a Saturday tasting menu, every dish carries the landscape of New Brunswick within it.

Executive Chef Isabelle Cormier-Leblanc brings 20 years of culinary craft — trained in Paris, seasoned in Halifax, rooted in Moncton — to every plate that leaves our kitchen.
